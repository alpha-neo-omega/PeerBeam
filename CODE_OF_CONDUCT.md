# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge
We — contributors and maintainers — pledge to make participation in PeerBeam a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body, disability,
ethnicity, gender identity, experience, nationality, appearance, race, religion,
or sexual identity.

## Our standards
Positive: welcoming language, respect for differing viewpoints, graceful
acceptance of constructive feedback, focus on what's best for the community.
Unacceptable: sexualized language/imagery, trolling or insults, harassment
(public or private), publishing others' private information, or other
unprofessional conduct.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code,
and may ban temporarily or permanently for inappropriate behavior. Report issues
privately to the maintainers (see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for the private
contact). Reports are handled confidentially.
